# Privacy Policy
Last Updated: March 2026

## TravelersWorkoutTracker Privacy Policy

TravelersWorkoutTracker ("the App") respects your privacy.

### 1. Data Collection

The App does **not** collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

All workout data, templates, sessions, and exercise history are stored locally on your device using Apple's built-in data storage systems.

The App does not require account creation.

The App does not use analytics, advertising frameworks, tracking technologies, or third-party data collection services.

### 2. Data Storage

All data entered into the App remains on your device.

If you choose to back up your device using iCloud or iTunes, your data may be included in those backups according to Apple’s policies.

The developer does not have access to your workout data.

### 3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with:
- Advertising networks
- Analytics providers
- External servers
- Social media platforms

### 4. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because no data is collected, no personal information is stored or transmitted.

### 5. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, security is managed by Apple's iOS platform protections.

### 6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Updates will be reflected by revising the “Last Updated” date above.
